Who We Are

About CACD & CTAF

CACD
Committee for Arts, Culture & Development

CACD is the apex body responsible for nurturing and promoting artistic, cultural, and developmental activities at Chennai Institute of Technology. We serve as the bridge between academic excellence and holistic student development, orchestrating programs that bring out the best in every student.

CTAF
CIT Technical & Arts Festival

CTAF is CIT's flagship annual festival — a grand convergence of technical innovation and artistic expression. Attracting thousands of participants from across Tamil Nadu, CTAF has grown into one of the most anticipated inter-collegiate festivals in the region.

◈
Our Vision

To cultivate a vibrant, inclusive cultural ecosystem at CIT where every student can discover, develop, and celebrate their unique talents — fostering leaders who shape society through arts, culture, and innovation.

◇
Our Mission

To organize transformative cultural and technical events, provide platforms for creative expression, recognize excellence, and build a community of confident, culturally aware students ready to contribute meaningfully to the world.
